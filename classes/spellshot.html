Ambi-Hextrous

The Fatemaker may equip a Spell in their inventory. Whenever the Spellshot presses their Action Skill Button, they cast that Spell.

Polymorph

The Fatemaker turns an enemy into a Skeep for a few seconds. If they are Immune, the Fatemaker instantly casts a free Spell on that enemy and gains two stacks of Spellweaving.

While Polymorph is active, the Fatemaker and their allies have a chance to cast a free Spell after dealing Gun Damage to the Polymorphed target. This effect is guaranteed the first time the Fatemaker deals Gun Damage. After the Fatemaker casts a Spell this way, they gain a stack of Spellweaving.

Cooldown: 28 seconds
Duration: 16 seconds
Spellweaving: Casting a Spell or reloading a weapon grants the Spellshot a stack of Spellweaving, increasing Spell Damage. Spellweaving stacks automatically decay after a few seconds.

Casting a Repeating Spell has a chance to award additional Spellweaving stacks with each Repeating Cast.

Spell Damage: +10% per stack
Stack Duration: 10 seconds
Max Stacks: 5
Repeating Cast Chance: 25%
